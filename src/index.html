Our cases

How to bring new customers and make your asset skyrocket through competition?

As there are many projects, the niche tends to be extremely competitive. In that regard, the key thing is to attract customers to the project with what they do best: being active participants.

So, customers can be rewarded for being active and joining a vibrant community of like-minded customers.

To achieve that, we launched a contest with a $1 mln prize, Bentley, and Bentayga (as a mega grand finale prize). Customers had to buy assets of the project on Binance DEX and keep these funds. As a result, this competition led to 1500 new users, assets worth $1 mln were sold, and the price of it soared.

How to build a better social media presence?

Creating a strong media strategy is really important for every project, and streaming services are not an exception. This time we cooperated with Vabble, promoting it on YouTube and Twitter and building a robust PR strategy. Our fruitful collaboration led to strong brand awareness, bringing 16 000 new customers and a group of 1 800 returning clients on the platform. In addition, the firm successfully gained more than $ 1 138 760 during its private round.

How to make your lead generation soar?

This time we worked with a world-class Digital Аssеts exchange, ABCC. The key target was to attract new clients, build brand awareness, and create a trusting foundation. Our team made it through articles and listings, defining site impracticalities, and improving conversion on the landing page. In addition, we advertised through Google Ads, provided AB landing page tests, and collected all stats devoted to the promotion of the project. As a result of our paid campaign, the company received more than 100 leads in 3 weeks.
